Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is dropshipping?

Dropshipping is a retail fulfillment method where a store doesn’t keep the products it sells in stock. Instead, when you buy a product, the store purchases the item from a third party and has it shipped directly to you.

How does the dropshipping process work?

When you place an order, we forward your order details to our supplier. They handle inventory, packaging, and shipping directly to your address.

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our products, add your desired items to the cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

How long will my order take to arrive?

Delivery times depend on the supplier and your location. Most orders are typically processed within 3-7 business days, but shipping can take longer during peak seasons.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within a specified period (e.g., 30 days) from the delivery date. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Please refer to our return policy for details.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via email, phone, or our contact form. We’re here to help!
